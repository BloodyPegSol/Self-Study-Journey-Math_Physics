       The quaternion group is the set $Q_{8} = \left\{ 1,-1,i,-i,j,-j,k,-k \right\}$ with product $\cdot$ computed as follows:
\begin{align*}
  1\cdot a= a\cdot 1 = a &, (-1)\cdot(-1) = 1, &  (-1)\cdot a = a\cdot (-1), &\forall a\in Q_{8}
\end{align*}
